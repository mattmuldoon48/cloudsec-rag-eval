# NIST SP 800-61 Rev. 3 Incident Response (official-source notes)

These notes are a concise local summary based on NIST SP 800-61 Rev. 3. They are not a copy of the full NIST publication.

Source URLs:
- https://csrc.nist.gov/pubs/sp/800/61/r3/final
- https://csrc.nist.gov/projects/incident-response

## Current Guidance

NIST SP 800-61 Rev. 3, published in April 2025, supersedes SP 800-61 Rev. 2. The updated guidance aligns incident response with the NIST Cybersecurity Framework 2.0 and treats incident response as part of broader cybersecurity risk management.

## Incident Response Outcomes

The guidance focuses on helping organizations prepare for incident response, reduce the number and impact of incidents, and improve detection, response, and recovery activities.

Incident response should be integrated across organizational operations rather than handled as an isolated technical process. Governance, preparation, detection, response, recovery, communication, and continuous improvement all contribute to an effective capability.

## Program Considerations

Organizations should define incident response roles, responsibilities, escalation paths, communication channels, and coordination mechanisms. They should also preserve useful evidence, analyze incident data, prioritize response activities, and incorporate lessons learned into risk management and future improvements.
